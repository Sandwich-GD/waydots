## Waybar

A status bar for wayland-based WMs (window managers), you will have to adjust the size tho,
depending on how large your monitor is. Currently, usually its set to most laptops resolution,
1366x768.

And just so you know, please put the pitchforks down and hear me out. I didnt want to make my own
waybar config, so i thought this one looked cool, and copied it, fixed it up, and called it my own.
So, tbh, this is just a copied config from pewdiepie, turned purple.
